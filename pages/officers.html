Meet the leadership of American Legion Post 300 — William J. Bordelon Post

🎖️ Our Namesake — SSgt. William James Bordelon

Staff Sergeant William James Bordelon was a United States Marine Corps combat engineer who was posthumously awarded the Medal of Honor for his actions during the Battle of Tarawa in 1943. Born in San Antonio, Texas, he gave his life in service to his country. Post 300 is proud to carry his name and honor his legacy in everything we do.
