Join American Legion Post 300

Serving veterans and the San Antonio community since 1995

🎖️ American Legion

For veterans who served during eligible wartime periods. Honor your service by joining your brothers and sisters.

⭐ Sons of The American Legion

For male descendants of veterans who served in the U.S. Armed Forces. Carry the legacy forward.

🌟 American Legion Auxiliary

For spouses, children, and family members of veterans. Serve alongside the post community.

🏅 Life Member

Lifetime membership for qualifying veterans. A permanent commitment to Post 300 and its mission.
