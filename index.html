Named in Honor of

Staff Sergeant William James Bordelon — Medal of Honor Recipient, Battle of Tarawa, WWII. His Medal of Honor was presented to his mother in San Antonio in 1944. One of Texas' most honored Marines.

Our mission is to serve veterans and their families, strengthen the local community, support youth programs, provide scholarships, assist veterans in need, and promote God, Country, and Community.
